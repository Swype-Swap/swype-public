# Supported Providers

SWYPE publicly discloses supported provider coverage while keeping route-selection and optimization logic private.

Supported provider usage may vary by:

- asset
- network
- route mode
- jurisdiction
- real-time availability
- operational conditions

## Supported Provider Surface

- ChangeNOW
- ChangeHero
- Godex
- SimpleSwap
- Swapuz
- Changelly
- CoinCraddle
- StealthEX
- LetsExchange
- Swapter

## Notes

- SimpleSwap may also support eligible fiat onramp and offramp flows where available.
- Public disclosure of provider coverage does not mean every provider is available for every route at all times.
- SWYPE does not publish provider ranking, planner weighting, or exact route-selection logic in this repository.

## Provider Support Relationship

SWYPE maintains active operational communication with supported providers and can escalate route or transaction issues for provider-side review when needed.

This does not mean SWYPE controls provider infrastructure or guarantees a specific resolution outcome. It does mean users and partners are not left on their own when a route requires deeper provider-side investigation.
